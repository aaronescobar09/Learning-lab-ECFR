What you are learning

The CFR is a map, not a pile of rules

Exam questions often ask where a topic lives. The winning move is to identify the level of the map before hunting for the exact words.

TitleBroad subject. Title 19 means Customs Duties.
ChapterAgency home. Chapter I is the CBP/Treasury chapter students see often.
PartTopic bucket. Part 111 is Customs Brokers.
SectionSpecific rule inside a part. 11.1 is one section in Part 11.

Worked example

Read 19 CFR 111.28(b) without guessing

19Title 19, Customs Duties
CFRCode of Federal Regulations
111Part 111, Customs Brokers
.28Section 28 inside Part 111
(b)Paragraph b inside the section

The exam habit is simple: say the levels out loud in order. Title, Part, Section, Paragraph. That keeps you from treating every number as the same kind of number.
