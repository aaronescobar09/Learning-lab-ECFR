Title 19 Navigation Quiz

A short retrieval-practice quiz for GTC certification students learning how to read CFR structure and recognize key Title 19 parts.

Exam constraint: use eCFR during study to learn the map. Do not train yourself to depend on live eCFR search during the exam.
Open the reference map Official eCFR Title 19
1. TitleTitle 19 means Customs Duties.
2. ChapterChapter I is the CBP/Treasury chapter students will see often.
3. PartPart numbers are topic buckets, such as 111 for Customs Brokers.

How to read a citation

Read 19 CFR 111.28(b) as: Title 19, Part 111, Section 28, paragraph b. The quiz asks you to retrieve that structure before checking feedback.

Quiz

Answer from memory first. After each choice, the feedback explains the navigation cue and the official-source basis.
